Counting Bicyclists in Tempe city (with Semi-supervised Reidentification) 3D map [Ongoing]


I found out that deploying market-available 'smart' bike counters is prohibitively expensive for the city. So, I am working on a method which is an interesting mix of new hardware and computer vision-based algorithms to count bicyclists at 27 different locations in Tempe. This project is still going on. Preliminary analysis shows about 15-20 times of cost savings.

3D reconstruction by NeRF and SfM from 360Â°camera images


Capturing a scene with Lidar inherently needs a large amount of data storage. So, in this study, I investigated an alternative approach to capture the 3D information via 360 videos. I particularly used Structure-from-Motion (SfM) and Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) for the task of 3D reconstruction. These techniques provide significant savings in terms of data storage requirements but there is a trade-off with dimensional accuracy. In my view, the selection of appropriate technology would be driven by its use case. For ex: for a simple task of visualization, SfM method could be preferred but if one needs precise measurement (of tiny or thin objects) then Lidar would be the choice.

Semi-supervised symbols detection in Piping and Instrumentation Drawings (P&IDs) [Paper under review]


Typically, P&IDs would have 100's of symbols depicting various equipment. Labelling more than 200 classes of symbols is a challenging task especially when some of them resemble a lot with one another. Hence, to reduce the annotation load, I used self-supervised contrastive learning and built a semi-supervised pipeline for symbol detection. The method achieved a Top-1 accuracy of 85.49% over 102 test classes.

Automated 2D building plans to 3D digital model [Journal paper under review; Conf paper]


Aim of this project is to speed up the conversion of a 2D CAD into a 3D BIM model. We found that the semantic segmentation-based approach is more performant than the typical bounding-based methods. In post-processing, we configured the linkage of neural network outputs to a 3D drafting software via Dynamo API.

Valve detection in P&IDs without human annotations: A synthetic data approach


Devised a synthetic data generation pipeline to create a labeled dataset without any human annotations. Experimentation revealed that the presence of look-alike symbols (i.e. hard negatives) helped in reducing false positives & increased the performance.
