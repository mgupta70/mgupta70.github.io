Hackathons

I. Kaggle: Predict CO2 Emissions in Rwanda

Secured 6th rank among 1453 teams in developing a regression model for predicting CO2 emissions in a time-series dataset.

(July - Aug 2023)



II. Kaggle: ICR-Identifying Age-Related Conditions

Achieved a Bronze medal for ranking in the Top 5% among 6430 teams.

Developed a multi-class classifier for predicting health conditions using 56 anonymous health markers in a heavily class-imbalanced dataset.

(Aug 2023)



III. Kaggle: DFL-Bundesliga Data Shootout

Developed an LSTM model for activity recognition during football matches. Performed feature engineering by assigning teams to players via K-means clustering and tracking football and players' locations. The developed LSTM model achieved 76% accuracy.

(July - Dec 2022)



IV. Autodesk: Student BIM Challenge

Designed a commercial complex (Architecture, Structure, and MEP) using Building Information Modeling (BIM) standards. The goal of the competition back in 2013 was to create awareness about BIM and increase its adoption. Presently, BIM is a mandatory requirement in many countries for any construction project.
