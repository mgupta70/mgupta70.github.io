About Me

My name is Mohit and I am a student of science. I hold a bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering and a master's degree in Mechanical Engineering. Currently, I am pursuing PhD from Arizona State University with research focus on Computer Vision and Machine Learning. I am being advised by Dr. Thomas Czerniawski, SSEBE , ASU.
