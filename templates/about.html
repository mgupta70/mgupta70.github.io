About Me



I am Mohit, a Ph.D. candidate in the Sustainable School of Engineering & the Built Environment (SSEBE) at Arizona State University. My research focusses on building end-to-end AI solutions for 2 indsutrial applications:
(A) Engineering Drawings Analysis and,
(B) Condition Monitoring of Assets using IoT sensors.

These end-to-end solutions go beyond training AI models. I address challenges such as:
(i) Negligible or sparsely annotated data,
(ii) Lack of standardization,
(iii) AI models being 'black-box' systems.

These are critical issues preventing not only building robust AI models but adversely impacts their integration with the AECO industry, a high-risk & low-margin industry. My advisors at ASU are - Dr. Thomas Czerniawski and Dr. Ricardo Eiris.
