About Me

My name is Mohit and I am a PhD student in Sustainable School of Engineering & Built Environment at Arizona State University. I am being advised by Dr. Thomas Czerniawski and Dr. Ricardo Eiris.

I am currently working on empirical modeling and predictive analytics to optimize maintenance time-cycles for large machinery like turbines. It involves developing temporal decay models quantifying the state of the machinery and using historical data to put in place contextual anomaly detection models to avoid sudden failures.
